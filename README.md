Life in Adventure: JS Edition
A choice-driven, text-based RPG built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. Navigate through a perilous fantasy world where every decision affects your reputation, wealth, and ultimate survival.

 Gameplay Features
Dynamic Storytelling: Experience a multi-chapter narrative that reacts to your choices.

Resource Management: Balance your Health, Gold, and Reputation to unlock special story paths and endings.

Turn-Based Combat: A strategic combat system featuring "Attack" and "Dodge/Counter" mechanics. Learn enemy patterns to survive encounters with Mist Panthers and the legendary Frost Dragon.

Inventory & Upgrades: Collect items like maps and soul gems, or visit the Mist Sorcerer to enchant your steel.

Multiple Endings: Your journey concludes with a unique title (e.g., The Hero King or The Wealthy Outcast) based on your moral choices throughout the game.

 Technical Highlights
State Management: Tracks player stats and inventory across different game scenes using a centralized JavaScript object.

Modular UI Updates: A custom logging system and UI updater that provides real-time feedback on stat gains and losses.

CSS Animations: Features a "Chapter Overlay" system for smooth transitions between story segments.
